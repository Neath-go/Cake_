WHO WE ARE

"Delight Bakery" has a rich history rooted in the heart of a quaint town called Willow Creek. Established in 1956 by the visionary baker, Josephine Miller, the bakery began as a modest storefront on Main Street. Josephine, known for her warm smile and unmatched baking skills inherited from her grandmother, started with a small menu of freshly baked bread and simple pastries.

Today, Delight Bakery continues to thrive under the leadership of Sarah, who took over as head baker and owner after Josephine's retirement. The bakery remains committed to its founding principles of quality, community, and creativity.
